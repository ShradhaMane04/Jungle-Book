Baloo

I am Baloo a bear who teaches Mowgli the law of the jungle.

Friends:Mowgli and Bagheera

Enemies:SherKhan,Tabaqui

Traits:Sleepy, strict, sweet

Baloo the bear helps to raise Mowgli. He is in charge of teaching all wolf cubs the Law of the Jungle and languages of all the animals. Baloo speaks up for Mowgli at the first Council Rock meeting when the pack is deciding whether Mowgli should stay or be cast out.Baloo is a bear who lives in the jungle and teaches the wolf cubs the languages of all the animals.
