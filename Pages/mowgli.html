Mowgli

Hey!! I am Mowgli a boy raised by wolves.

Friends:Baloo and Bagheera

Enemies:SherKhan and Tabaqui

Traits:Fearless, intelligent, quick on his feet

Mowgli is a little boy who is adopted by a wolf pack and raised in the jungle.a fearless boy who escaped being eaten by Shere Khan the tiger, is allowed to grow up with a wolf pack. He learns to communicate with nearly any animal in the jungle and eventually lures Shere Khan to his death.
