Tabaqui is a jackal who antagonizes the Free People wolf pack.Tabaqui is the golden jackal from the jungle who is Shere Khan's only companion. His role is to serve as Shere Khan's spy as well as his go-between with other jungle creatures. His other role, in the literary sense, is as a foil (or opposite) of the more admirable animals in the jungle. He is not popular with either humans or animals.
